Singapore Sling

As your Attorney I advise you to drink at least three.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 oz gin
  • 1/4 oz Benedictine
  • 1/4 oz Grand Marnier
  • 1/4 oz Heering cherry liqueur
  • 1 oz pineapple juice
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1 dash of Angostrura bitters
  • Clib soda, chilled, to top
  • Garnish: orange slice
  • Garnish: cherry

Steps

  1. Add liquors and juice in a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a highball glass over fresh ice, top with club soda
  3. Garnish with orange slice and a cherry.
