Bloody Mary

Getting blind drunk together is really the only way to say farewell

Ingredients

  • 2oz Vodka
  • 4oz tomato juice
  • Celery salt
  • 2 Dashes Tabasco
  • 2 Dashes Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 Dashes bitters
  • Garnish lemon wedge
  • Garnish lime wedge
  • Garnish 2 Olives
  • Garnish celery stalk

Steps

  1. Place Celery salt onto a plate
  2. Rub lip of glass with a lemon or lime wedge
  3. Roll tthe outer edge of the glass in celery salt until fully coated
  4. Fill the glass with ice
  5. Add Vodka and tomato juice
  6. Add Tabasco, Worcestershire, and bitters
  7. Stir
  8. Add garnishes to glass
  9. Enjoy
